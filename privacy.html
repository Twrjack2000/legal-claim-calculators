Overview

Legal Claim Calculators ("we," "our," or "the site") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Short version: All calculator inputs you enter are processed entirely in your browser. We do not receive, store, or transmit any numbers you enter into our legal calculators.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email address, or any personally identifying information
  • Any numbers you enter into our calculators (wages, medical bills, income, etc.)
  • Any legal details about your specific case or situation
  • Your precise location

Information Automatically Collected

Like all websites, our web server and analytics tools may automatically receive:

  • IP Address — Used for general location (country/region), fraud prevention, and server logs. Not linked to your calculator inputs.
  • Browser and Device Information — Browser type, operating system, screen size. Used for technical optimization.
  • Pages Visited and Time Spent — Collected via Google Analytics to understand which tools are most used and how to improve the site.
  • Referring URL — The website that linked you to us, if any.

Cookies

We use cookies for the following limited purposes:

  • Google Analytics — Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics. You can opt out at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.
  • Google AdSense — Our advertising partner uses cookies to serve relevant ads. Google's advertising privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

We do not use cookies for tracking personal information, profiling, or retargeting based on your calculator usage.

Google AdSense and Third-Party Advertising

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and web beacons to serve ads based on your past visits to this site and other sites on the internet. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads.

We do not share your calculator inputs or any personal data with advertisers. Advertisers do not have access to any information you enter into our tools.

Children's Privacy

Our site is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

Since we collect minimal personal data, most access and deletion requests are not applicable. However:

  • EU/EEA Users (GDPR): You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of any personal data we hold. Because we collect essentially no personal data, there is generally nothing to access or delete.
  • California Residents (CCPA): We do not sell personal information. We do not share personal information with third parties for their direct marketing purposes.

Changes to This Policy

Contact
